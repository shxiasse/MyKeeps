## 🇬🇧 English

### In Short
**MyKeeps does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All your data stays on your device.**

### Data Collection
MyKeeps is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information or user data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage data or analytics are gathered
- No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties
- No tracking or advertising identifiers are used

### Local Data Storage
All data you create in MyKeeps (themes, entries, photos, and media) is stored exclusively on your local device using:

- SwiftData for structured data storage
- Local file system for media files

You have complete control over your data. You can delete any content at any time through the app.

### In-App Purchases
MyKeeps offers in-app purchases through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple, and we do not have access to your payment information.

### Third-Party Services
MyKeeps does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

### Children's Privacy
MyKeeps does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

### Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

### Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

**Email:** shxiasse@outlook.de
